    — Knowledge Base

    Care Guides

    💧
    Founding Setup
    Fill a test tube ⅓ with water, seal with cotton, and place your queen inside. Keep in darkness at 24–27°C for best founding success.
    🌡️
    Temperature & Humidity
    Most species thrive at 24–28°C with 50–70% humidity. Use a heat mat on one side only, leaving a temperature gradient.
    🍯
    Feeding
    Offer a sugar source (honey water, fruit) and a protein source (small insects). Feed 2–3× per week once workers emerge.
    🏠
    Nesting
    Upgrade from test tube to formicarium when workers number 20–50. Use Fluon on outworld walls to prevent escapes.

    All orders are processed within 1–3 business days. Orders placed on weekends or holidays are processed the next business day. Live ant shipments are only dispatched Monday through Wednesday to ensure your colony doesn't sit in a warehouse over the weekend.

    Shipping Rates

    Standard shipping is $9.99 on all orders. Orders over $75 qualify for free shipping automatically — no code needed. Expedited shipping options may be available at checkout for an additional fee.

    Live Arrival Guarantee

    We guarantee that all live animals arrive alive and in good health. In the event of a DOA (dead on arrival), contact us within 2 hours of delivery with a clear photo. We will reship or refund at no charge to you.

    Packaging

    All live colonies are shipped in insulated boxes with heat packs in cold weather and cool packs in hot weather. Test tube setups are secured to prevent movement during transit. We use overnight or 2-day shipping for all live animal orders.
